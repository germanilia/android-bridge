# Personas — Meeting Capture Feature

## P1 — Owner-User

### Profile
- Uses a Samsung Galaxy phone and Mac as a personal continuity setup.
- Attends meetings, lectures, demos, and conversations where notes are useful.
- Wants the phone to capture audio/photos and the Mac to process everything locally.
- Values privacy, low friction, and practical output over cloud collaboration features.

### Goals
- Start recording quickly from the phone.
- Take timestamped photos during a session without interrupting recording.
- Receive useful transcript, summary, speaker-labeled notes, and image references on the Mac.
- Save notes locally and share them through native Mac sharing.
- Avoid leaving private recordings and photos on the phone after transfer.

### Pain Points
- Manual note taking misses details.
- Photos of boards/slides are hard to reconnect to the spoken context later.
- Cloud transcription tools raise privacy concerns.
- Long recordings are risky if connectivity drops or the app goes to background.

### Success Criteria
- Capture works while the phone screen is off.
- No recording is lost during ordinary reconnects.
- Notes are produced locally on the Mac.
- Speaker labels can be renamed after the session.
- Images appear in the right chronological place in the notes.
